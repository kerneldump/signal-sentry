# Product Guide - Signal Sentry

## Initial Concept
A CLI tool designed to monitor and display real-time signal statistics from a T-Mobile home internet gateway, helping users optimize their connectivity.

## Target Audience
- **T-Mobile Home Internet Customers:** Users looking for an easy way to find the optimal placement for their gateway.
- **Power Users:** Individuals who require detailed monitoring of signal stability and performance over time.
- **Network Enthusiasts:** Users interested in technical details like specific 5G/4G bands, tower sectors (gNBID/CID), and signal quality metrics.

## Product Goals
- **Real-Time Visibility:** Provide a clear, immediate view of critical signal metrics (RSRP, SINR, Bands, etc.) to understand current connection quality.
- **Placement Optimization:** Enable users to move their gateway and see instant feedback to identify the highest-performing location in their home.
- **Stability Troubleshooting:** Offer the ability to log signal data over extended periods to identify patterns, drops, or tower switching issues.

## Core Features
- **Color-Coded Dashboard:** A live console output that uses visual cues (Green/Yellow/Red) to indicate signal health at a glance.
- **Historical Logging:** Support for exporting collected metrics to CSV or JSON files for long-term analysis and graphing.
- **"Find Best Spot" Mode:** A specialized interface or mode focused on placement, providing high-visibility indicators for signal improvements.
- **Detailed Signal Metrics:** Comprehensive display of 4G/5G bands, signal power, quality, and tower identification information.
